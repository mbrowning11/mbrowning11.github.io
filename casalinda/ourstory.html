OUR STORY

MEET LINDA

The heart and soul behind Casa Linda, Linda grew up in a small town in Mexico, where her love for cooking blossomed in her family’s kitchen. Inspired by her mother’s traditional recipes and the vibrant flavors of Mexican cuisine, Linda dreamed of sharing her heritage with others. She opened Casa Linda, bringing the warmth and authenticity of her hometown to every dish served. With a dedication to quality ingredients and a welcoming atmosphere, Linda’s passion shines through in every meal, making Casa Linda a beloved spot in the community.
