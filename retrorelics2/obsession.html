Calvin Klein Obsession Fragrance

Launched in 1985, Calvin Klein's Obsession became an iconic scent of the decade, symbolizing passion and sophistication. With its warm, spicy, and oriental notes, it appealed to men and women alike, and its bold advertising campaigns helped solidify the brand's reputation in the luxury fragrance market. Obsession remains a classic, epitomizing 1980s glamour and allure.
